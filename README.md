

## Aggie Code of Honor

"As an Aggie, I am committed to honesty and integrity in all of my academic activities. I will not engage in any dishonorable conduct, nor will I tolerate it in others."

## Academic Integrity

The materials in this repository are provided for educational purposes and personal reference only. Collaboration and discussion are encouraged in the learning process, but it's important to adhere to the Aggie Code of Honor. Please use these materials responsibly and ethically. Copying or presenting this work as your own without proper citation not only disrespects the efforts put into these works but also violates the academic integrity policies of Texas A&M University.

### Final Thoughts

I encourage you to approach this repository as a learning tool. Engage with the material, challenge yourself to understand the concepts, and apply what you learn in your own unique way. If you have any questions or wish to discuss the content further, I am open to academic discourse that fosters a deeper understanding of the subject matter.
